# Privacy Policy for DeUTC

**Last Updated:** June 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how DeUTC ("we", "our", or "the extension") handles user information and data.

## 1. No Data Collection or Storage
DeUTC is designed to respect user privacy.
* **No Personal Data:** The extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).
* **No Browsing History:** We do not track, log, or store your browsing history.
* **No Data Harvesting:** The extension does not inspect, read, or process web page content for any purpose other than converting UTC timestamps to your local timezone in real-time.

## 2. Local Processing Only
All text processing and timezone conversions are performed locally on your device within your web browser. 
* No data is transmitted to external servers.
* No analytics or tracking libraries are integrated into the extension.

## 3. Explanation of Permissions Required
To function properly, DeUTC requires the following permission:
* **`<all_urls>` (Host Permission):** Since system status pages are hosted across many custom and third-party domains (e.g., `status.github.com`, `status.stripe.com`), the extension needs permission to load on these pages. 
* **Scope Restriction:** The extension's logic is optimized to only execute on pages whose URLs match "status" keywords to ensure it remains lightweight and secure.

## 4. Third-Party Services
DeUTC does not connect to or share information with any third-party services, APIs, or servers.
